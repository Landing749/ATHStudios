This Privacy Policy explains how the QR Attendance System collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our Software. We are committed to safeguarding the privacy of students, educators, and institutions. Please read this document carefully.
01

Introduction

The QR Attendance System ("Software," "we," "us," or "our") is a desktop application designed to assist educational institutions in tracking student attendance using QR code technology. This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the Software, including teachers, administrators, and any institution deploying the Software.

This policy is designed to comply with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) of the Philippines and its Implementing Rules and Regulations. As a data processor, we are committed to ensuring that any personal data handled through the Software is processed lawfully, fairly, and transparently.

The institution operating the Software acts as the data controller and bears primary responsibility for compliance with applicable data protection laws with respect to student Attendance Data.

02

Data We Collect

The Software collects and processes the following categories of data:

Data Type Description Status
Student Names Names loaded from your institution's Excel roster file Required
QR Code Content Decoded QR data used to identify students during scanning Required
Attendance Timestamps Date and time records of each student's attendance scan Automatic
Presence Status Present/Absent classification per student per session Automatic
Device Hardware ID Hardware fingerprint used for license validation only Automatic
License Key Your unique activation key for Software authentication Required
IP Address Collected by third-party services during cloud sync operations Automatic
Grade & Section Class metadata entered by the user when submitting SF2 reports Optional

We do not collect government identification numbers, financial information, biometric data (other than QR codes as designated by your institution), health information, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is listed above.

03

How We Use Your Data

Data collected by the Software is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Attendance Tracking: Recording and displaying daily student attendance records within the Software.
  • Report Generation: Producing attendance summaries, SF2 forms, and exportable reports for administrative purposes.
  • Cloud Backup: Optionally uploading attendance files to Google Drive for secure backup at the user's discretion.
  • Administrative Submission: Sending SF2 attendance records to authorized school administrators via Cloudinary.
  • License Validation: Verifying that your License Key is valid and active via Firebase to prevent unauthorized use.
  • Hardware Detection: Associating your device's hardware ID with your License Key to prevent unauthorized license sharing.
  • Software Improvement: Anonymized error data may be used to diagnose and fix technical issues.

We do not use student Attendance Data for advertising, profiling, resale, or any purpose unrelated to the operation of the Software.

04

Data Storage & Security

Attendance Data is stored primarily on your local device in Excel (.xlsx) format within the application's designated folders. The Software implements the following security practices:

  • Local files are stored in user-specific directories under your operating system's standard user profile paths.
  • Cloud uploads to Cloudinary are transmitted over HTTPS using TLS encryption.
  • Google Drive backups use OAuth 2.0 authentication with read/write-limited scope.
  • Firebase license data is stored under access-controlled rules limiting data visibility.
  • No plaintext passwords are stored by the Software.
You are responsible for securing the device on which the Software is installed. The Developer is not liable for data loss or unauthorized access resulting from inadequate device security on your end.

In the event of a data breach affecting Cloud Services used by the Software, we will make reasonable efforts to notify affected users in a timely manner, in accordance with applicable law.

05

Third-Party Services

The Software integrates with the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy, which we encourage you to review:

Firebase (Google LLC)
Used for license key validation, active status monitoring, and SF2 metadata storage. Data is subject to Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy.
Cloudinary
Used for secure transmission of SF2 Excel files to school administrators. Files are uploaded using signed credentials. Subject to Cloudinary's Privacy Policy at cloudinary.com/privacy.
Google Drive (Google LLC)
An optional feature for backing up attendance files to the user's own Google Drive account. The Software requests only the minimum necessary scope (drive.file). Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services. Your use of cloud features constitutes acceptance of the respective third-party terms and privacy policies.

06

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data to any third party. Data may be shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • School Administration: SF2 attendance files are transmitted to authorized school administrators as explicitly directed by the user through the Software's "Send to Administration" feature.
  • Service Providers: Third-party cloud services (Firebase, Cloudinary, Google) process data solely to provide their respective services and are contractually restricted from using your data for other purposes.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required to do so by law, court order, or governmental authority, or if we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.
  • Business Transfer: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user data may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to the same privacy commitments.

Legal Basis (Philippines)

Under the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173), the processing of personal data by this Software is based on the following lawful grounds:

  • Contractual Necessity: Processing is necessary to fulfill the license agreement and provide the Software's core attendance-tracking functionality.
  • Legitimate Interest: License validation and hardware detection serve the legitimate interest of preventing unauthorized use and software piracy.
  • Consent: Where required (e.g., optional cloud backup features), processing is performed based on the user's informed and freely given consent, which may be withdrawn at any time.
  • Compliance with Legal Obligation: Processing may be necessary to comply with applicable laws and regulations in the Philippines.

The National Privacy Commission (NPC) of the Philippines serves as the supervisory authority for data protection matters. You have the right to lodge a complaint with the NPC if you believe your data rights have been violated.
